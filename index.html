Mixed Berry Smoothie Recipe



Ingredients:



  • 1 cup of Frozen Mixed Berries or fresh blueberries/strawberries/raspberries
  • 1/2 - 1 1/3 cup of Cranberry Juice - Sugar Free
  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 cup of baby spinach
  • 1 tbsp of oats
  • 1 tsp of almonds
  • 1 tsp of chia seeds
  • 1 tsp of flax seeds
  • 1/4 cup of Ice (optional... if you are going to use ice put ice in first)
  • 1 serving of protein powder (at least 20g... add second)

Instructions:

  1. Put 1/2 cup of mixed berries in the blender
  2. Chop up 1/2 a banana in put in the blender
  3. Throw in 1/2 cup of spinach in the blender
  4. Add about 1 cup of the cranberry juice to the blender
  5. Press the blend button on the smoothie to blend the smoothie for about 30 secs - 1 min
  6. Add to bowl
  7. Add garnishes (i.e. fresh cut strawberries, blueberries, oats, flax seeds, almonds, chia seeds, 1/2 banana, etc.) and serve
